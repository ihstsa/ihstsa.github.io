Bringing students and together.

We are a club of students learning about STEM, with the goal of competing at the Technology Student Association state and national competition. We do lots of fun stuff like building catapults, coding video games, and creating amazing pictures (you use Photoshop)—and lots more!

About Us

It turns out that we're better at catapult building, fixing outer-space related problems with biotechnology, and debating uses of technology than a lot of people. Join us!
